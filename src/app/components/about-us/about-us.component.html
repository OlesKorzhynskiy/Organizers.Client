About Us

SkinnyMe Tea(SMT) is an Australian based company whose mission is to help people like you achieve their health and wellness goals.  

Founded in 2012. Gretta started the business from her home in Melbourne, combining her passion  for tea and detoxing into a single product, creating the world’s first ‘Teatox’. 
The popular  two-step program combines a Morning and Evening Cleanse products as well as Eating and Exercise tips to achieve the results you have been looking for* All of our products are Made in Australia from the highest quality herb and extracts. 

The team of health professionals in nutrition, naturopathy, and exercise are here to provide the support you need. We are proud to have assisted over 350,000 people just like you to achieve their health goals, and it is success stories such as these that motivate our team to provide the best product and service possible to make sure you do too.
